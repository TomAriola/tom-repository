Introduction

The NFL draft, officially known as the Annual Player Selection Meeting, is an annual event which serves as the most common source of player recruitment in the National Football League.

For the first time in the common draft era,the 2025 draft commenced with all teams holding their original selections in the first round. The Houston Texans and Los Angeles Rams later traded out of the first round after the draft had begun. This draft is also notable for lacking selections outside of NCAA Division I.
